Kelly Tidwell

Master Stylist / Colorist

Hi, I'm Kelly Tidwell! I began my career in 2004 in Germantown, TN. Today, I live and work in the charming town of Collierville, TN with my husband, Chad, and our daughters, Sayler and Tatum.

I am a Keune Color Artist and Redken Specialist, with a passion for creating soft, seamless, and naturally blended color. Blonding is my specialty, and I love enhancing each guest's look with customized cuts, color, and styling. Above all, I believe healthy hair is the foundation of every beautiful result — no matter the length or shade.

As an independent stylist, you can reach me directly by text at 901.601.7293 to schedule a consultation or book an appointment.
