Raghan Thorn

Stylist

Raghan is a Collierville native who loves the beauty industry. She is outgoing and loves to get to know her clients. She often refers to her clients as her friends. She has 13 years of experience behind the chair and travels often to attend classes to further her education.

Raghan specializes in hair coloring & extension services. She specifically loves creating dimensional blondes, brunettes & rich reds. She added hair extensions to her list of services back in 2013 and continues to stay up to date with the latest trends.

She also provides other services such as hair cutting, keratin treatments & event styles. Raghan uses Keune Hair Cosmetics, Unite styling products as well as a few favorites from other lines.

She is certified with four different extension companies including Kacey Welch Silk Wefts & Habit Hand Tied Extensions.

When Raghan isn't working, she is staying busy with her family. She is a firefighter's wife and a mom to two precious boys! In her free time, her hobbies include traveling, entertaining & decorating!
