Hollie Skaggs

Stylist

Hollie is a fun loving stylist who makes sure you enjoy the time in her chair from start to finish.

Hollie has been behind the chair for over 20 years. She stays up to date on all of the latest trends and techniques. Hollie specializes in haircolor and haircutting.

She is a certified color specialist and uses Keune Hair Color. She is also certified in Bellami Hair Extensions. Not only will she provide you with the hair of your dreams, she will also make you laugh with her witty sense of humor and will always tell you the truth!

If you're looking for a trendy stylist with experience, who loves to have fun, Hollie is your girl!
