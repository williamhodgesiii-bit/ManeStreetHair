Karen Tabor

Stylist & Owner

With over 25 years in the salon industry, as owner and stylist, Karen decided she wanted to open a salon in Historic Downtown Collierville where clients get to experience the charm and character of this unique little town.

She (along with the help of family and friends) has created a warm and inviting atmosphere where you will feel welcomed and appreciated by the end of your first visit. Your time with her is more than just a haircut.

When Karen is not in the salon making over her customers, she is transforming outdated houses, starting her Airbnb ventures at Pickwick Lake, traveling on weekends to support and cheer on her son who is a sprinter on the Samford University track and field team, relaxing on the water at Pickwick Lake and last but not least, playing USTA league tennis around the Memphis area.

At this time Karen is no longer taking new clients. There are several stylists at Mane Street that would be happy to accommodate you.
