Karen Tabor

Stylist & Owner

Karen has over 30 years of experience behind the chair and a long-standing career as a successful salon owner. Her passion for the beauty industry is evident in every client she serves, combining technical expertise with a natural creative eye to deliver personalized, confidence-boosting results.

Beyond the salon, Karen is a true creative at heart. She enjoys remodeling and updating homes throughout her community, bringing the same vision and attention to detail to her projects as she does to her craft. In her free time, she loves spending quality time with her family, playing golf, and embracing the outdoors whenever she can.

Karen’s dedication to her clients, her business, and her creative pursuits continues to fuel her love for what she does every day.

At this time, Karen is not accepting new clients.
